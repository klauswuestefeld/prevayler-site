ShareThePrevalenceBase

This is a rough rendering of a page from the old Prevayler wiki. Please see the new wiki for current documentation.

If you want a very simple backup procedure in your prevalent system, you can put the PrevalenceBase directory under a network share (NFS, SMB, etc) and have a backup machine read the snapshots and command-logs, without stopping the system, while having the system in a very consistent post-recovery state. And, the best part: you don't even need those expensive backup systems. Just a simple tar+gzip to a tape or other reliable media will do just fine.

-- CarlosVillela
