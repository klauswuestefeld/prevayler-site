Hi Ron, please tell us a bit about yourself.  @:)

Ron Lancaster a.k.a. nyven is an experienced programmer and software development manager with a Masters of Science in Software Engineering and a B.A. in Business Administration.

WidgetWeb is the first application written by Ron to use the Prevayler engine. The original reason to use Prevayler is that it was extremely fast in the prototypes. However, after further use it quickly became evident that the lack of need for a data objects layer to manage translating method calls to SQL dramatically speeds development and greatly simplifies the architecture. The point is reduced development cost.
