AccidentalDifficulty

This is a rough rendering of a page from the old Prevayler wiki. Please see the new wiki for current documentation.

Complexity arising from the limitations of computing environments.

IOException handlers, RemoteException handlers and persistence code are typical examples.

It is Prevayler's goal to take care of all accidental difficulty related to persistence and fault-tolerance, thus providing BusinessObjectParadise.

Contrast with: EssentialDifficulty
