What is EMS? 
EMS means Electronics Manufacturing Services. 
 
"The Electronic Manufacturing Services sector is currently one of the world’s most dynamic industries. With an average growth of 25% per year, the EMS industry is very attractive for investors. Technology Forecasters, an independent analyst estimated the EMS Industry at $73.2 billion in 1999 with a forecast of $149.4 billion for 2003. Bearn, Stearns & Co Inc. estimates the total available market at $500 billion, meaning that only 15% is outsourced at this time."
